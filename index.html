Mehedi Marof

Investigative journalist working at the intersection of accountability, data, politics and public-interest reporting.

More ↓

Mehedi Marof is an investigative journalist from Dhaka, currently based in Washington, D.C., and reporting with the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism at the University of Maryland. He is also pursuing a master of journalism (M.J.) there. He previously was a reporter with the non-profit investigative newsroom Netra News in Dhaka. His freelance reporting has appeared in publications including Al Jazeera, NBC News, Rest of World, The Diplomat, Dialogue Earth, Scroll, Maryland Matters, and The Wire. He graduated bachelors with summa cum laude in 2023 from the University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh, majoring in journalism, and received both the Vice Chancellor’s Award and the Dean’s Award. He also was a research assistant at the Center for Sustainable Development in ULAB, and contributed to a study on coastal fishers.
